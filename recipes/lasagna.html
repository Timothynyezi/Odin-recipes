Lasagna Recipe

lasagna is a classic Italian al forno dish that generally consists of alternative layesers of paster sheets and various filling.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Have all of the ingredients ready.
  2. Make the meat sauce twice.
  3. Cook or do not cook your noodles.
  4. Prep the cheese and finish the sauce.
  5. Assemble the layers.
  6. Cover with foil.
  7. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
